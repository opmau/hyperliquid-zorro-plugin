### [hashtag](#internal-trading-sessions) **Internal Trading Sessions**

Internal pricing is used from Friday 17:00 to Sunday 17:00 ET, or at any time there is a gap of more than 30 seconds between external oracle datapoints. This ensures continuous pricing coverage across all sessions, including after-hours. You can read more about the internal pricing mechanism [herearrow-up-right](https://docs.trade.xyz/xyz-perps-specification/equity-perpetuals#internal-pricing).
