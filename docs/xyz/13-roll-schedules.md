#### [hashtag](#roll-schedule) Roll Schedule:

The following table denotes the active contract month for markets that reference futures-based price feeds (e.g., CL, Copper, Natural Gas), at the start of each month. The roll period is the 5th-10th business day of the month, wherein the reference contract transitions from the month-start active contract to the next active contract.
